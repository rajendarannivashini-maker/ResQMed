# ResQMed

ResQMed

AI-Powered Emergency Healthcare Assistance Platform

Abstract

ResQMed is an intelligent emergency healthcare assistance platform designed to help people quickly access critical medical resources during emergencies. The system provides real-time information about hospital bed availability, blood availability, ambulance services, and nearby healthcare facilities. By integrating healthcare providers, blood banks, ambulance operators, and patients into a single platform, ResQMed aims to reduce emergency response time and improve access to life-saving medical services.

⸻

Problem Statement

During medical emergencies, patients and their families often struggle to find available hospital beds, blood donors, blood banks, and ambulance services. The lack of a centralized platform leads to delays in treatment, increased stress, and, in some cases, loss of lives. ResQMed addresses this issue by providing a unified platform that offers real-time healthcare resource availability and emergency assistance.

⸻

Objectives

* Provide real-time hospital bed availability information.
* Enable quick access to blood donor and blood bank details.
* Facilitate ambulance booking and tracking.
* Help users locate nearby hospitals and healthcare facilities.
* Reduce emergency response time through efficient resource management.
* Improve communication between patients and healthcare providers.
